Backspace is a company that builds tech companies

Our goal is to build ideas into successful companies. We combine skills and capital to rapidly execute on an emerging technology. It’s a place where multiple ideas are build to product/market fit. After this seed stage, they debut as independent startups.

Technology pushes humanity forward

One thing in history has consistently increased human wellbeing. The progress of technology. It’s what pushes humanity forward. That’s why we use technology to make the world a little better. It’s what we call ‘creating positive impact’. To disrupt is to change things for the better.

Using technology to create positive impact

Experience has shown us repeatable elements in building a tech startup. Based on these insights we’ve developed an analytical process, which we call the ‘venture creation flow’. Success and failure will continu to improve the steps in building a company. We will build several companies in parallel, but always with a dedicated founder from our studio.

The perfect co-founder of a seed stage startup

As a studio we co-found a startup with an entrepreneur who is a specialist in his field. We build the startup from idea to product/market fit over a period of 24 months. This makes them ready for growth investment (Series A). After this period we deliver an independent startup with traction, a complete team and a working product.
